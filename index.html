03

Automation & Information Management

I automate what teams repeat hundreds of times ‚Äî and shouldn't. Most AEC problems are not model problems. They are information problems.

I enhance design software using Dynamo and Grasshopper, along with C# for Revit API. Focus on QA/QC model validations for Revit and Civil 3D models, combining multiple data flows, reporting in PowerBI with rule-based checks. Also, design and coordination automation, infrastructure-scale workflows.

Using connections with all project information via PowerBI, PowerApps, and Microsoft Flows to automate all types of information flows. ISO 19650 turned into working processes, model-to-data pipelines, dashboards for decision-makers, automated reviews, approvals, and audits.
